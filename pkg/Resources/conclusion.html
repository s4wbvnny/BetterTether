Getting Started

  1. Connect your Android phone to your Mac via USB-C
  2. On your phone, enable USB Tethering (Settings → Tethering → USB Tethering)
  3. That's it! Your internet will route through the phone automatically.

Verify Installation

sudo launchctl list | grep princePal

Logs

tail -f /var/log/bettertether.log

Uninstall

Run the uninstall script or manually:

sudo launchctl bootout system /Library/LaunchDaemons/com.princePal.bettertether.plist
sudo rm -f /Library/LaunchDaemons/com.princePal.bettertether.plist
sudo rm -f /usr/local/bin/bettertether
sudo rm -rf /etc/bettertether
