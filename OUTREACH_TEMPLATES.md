## 3. The "Native RNDIS for M-series" (Solution-Focused / Tech-Savvy)
**Target**: Developers or power users who understand the technical gap.

> If you're tired of HoRNDIS failing and don't want to mess with kernel extensions, I just released **BetterTether**.
>
> It's a lightweight userspace daemon that handles the RNDIS handshake natively via `libusb` and pipes it through a `utun` interface. 
> 🔗 **GitHub**: https://github.com/s4wbvnny/BetterTether
>
> **Quick Facts:**
> - Zero Kexts / Zero SIP changes.
> - Verified working on macOS Sequoia/Tahoe.
> - Includes a specialized Samsung Galaxy MAC-randomization workaround.
>
> Would love to get some feedback from the community on performance! ⚡
